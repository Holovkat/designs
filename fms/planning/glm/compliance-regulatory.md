# FMS Compliance and Regulatory Requirements

## 1. Transportation Regulations

### 1.1 Hours of Service (HOS) Compliance
**Requirement**: As a Compliance Officer, I want automated HOS tracking so that driver work hours comply with federal regulations.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- AC1: Automatic HOS calculation based on GPS and vehicle telemetry data
- AC2: Real-time alerts when drivers approach legal driving limits
- AC3: Electronic logging device (ELD) integration with FMCSA compliance
- AC4: Automated duty status switching (driving, on-duty, off-duty, sleeper berth)
- AC5: Digital driver logs with electronic signature capture
- AC6: HOS exception handling with approval workflows
- AC7: Regulatory report generation (daily logs, weekly summaries, monthly reports)

### 1.2 Vehicle Inspection Requirements
**Requirement**: As a Fleet Safety Manager, I want digital vehicle inspection management so that regulatory inspections are properly documented.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- AC1: Digital pre-trip and post-trip inspection forms with photo capture
- AC2: Automated inspection reminders based on mileage and time intervals
- AC3: Defect tracking with repair workflow integration
- AC4: DVIR (Driver Vehicle Inspection Report) electronic submission
- AC5: Inspection history analytics and trend identification
- AC6: Regulatory agency audit preparation tools
- AC7: Out-of-service criteria enforcement with automatic vehicle blocking

### 1.3 Driver Qualification Files
**Requirement**: As an HR Manager, I want digital driver qualification management so that all driver documentation remains current.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- AC1: Digital driver file storage with document expiration tracking
- AC2: Automated renewal reminders for licenses, medical cards, certifications
