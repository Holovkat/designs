# Privacy & Retention (Draft)

## Retention
- Orders: 2 years (auditing), then anonymize customer fields.
- GPS/Telemetry: 30 days online; aggregated analytics retained 1 year.
- POD (images/signatures): 7 years with legal hold support.
- Alerts/Logs: 90 days online, archive 1 year.

## Data Subject Rights
- Access/erasure requests logged; responses within legal SLA (e.g., 45 days CPRA).
- Redaction pipeline for PII in logs and analytics.

## Security
- Field-level encryption for PII; KMS-managed keys with rotation.
- RBAC and tenant isolation; least privilege default roles.
